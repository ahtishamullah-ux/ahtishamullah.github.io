Ahtisham Ullah

Mechanical Engineer · Green Hydrogen Researcher · Management Specialist

ℹ️ About: Official professional biography of Ahtisham Ullah, a Pakistani-Italian Mechanical Engineer based in Lecce, Italy, specializing in green hydrogen research.

Ahtisham Ullah (born in Gujranwala, Pakistan) is a Pakistani-Italian mechanical engineer, researcher, and management engineering specialist based in Lecce, Italy. He holds a Master of Science in Management Engineering from the University of Salento and a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore.

He is recognized for his work on green hydrogen production, sustainable industrial processes, and supply chain optimization, with approximately three years of experience spanning Pakistan, Italy, and Spain.

Ullah is a recipient of the AMAL Academy Career Preparation Fellowship, funded by Stanford University, and a participant in the European Union Erasmus+ programme.

Biography

Ahtisham Ullah was born and raised in Gujranwala, Pakistan. He completed his matriculation at Govt. P.B Model High School (2015, 90%) and F.Sc Pre-Engineering at Superior Group of Colleges, Gujranwala (2017, 88%). From an early stage, he demonstrated a strong interest in engineering, sustainability, and problem-solving.

Career

Logistics & Supply Chain Specialist (2024)

Under the Erasmus+ programme, worked at DPoint Group in Barcelona, Spain — managing inventory with SAP/Excel and coordinating logistics using TMS and CargoSmart.

Engineer Intern — Green Hydrogen (2024)

At SKEM@ S.R.L, Brindisi, Italy, in collaboration with the University of Salento's Management and Engineering Lab, conducted techno-economic analysis for 5MW PEM and AWE electrolyzer plants and 8MW solar systems.

Mechanical Procurement Engineer (2022–2023)

At HAROBANX, handled procurement of mechanical equipment, supplier evaluations, and inventory management.

Erection Engineer (2021–2022)

Led erection of Dyeing & Finishing Department at Kamal Textile Limited, working with world-class machinery from SCLAVOS, BRUCKNER, FERRARO, LAFER, SANTEX RIMAR, and CORINO. Implemented Total Productive Maintenance (TPM) achieving MTTR/MTBF KPIs.
